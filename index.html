About Me

Full-stack Developer

I’m a Full-Stack developer with a background in the production and fabrication industries. I know what it takes to bring a project from its beginning and build it up to be client ready. In my role, I was the initial point of contact for clients. I analyzed their requests and oversaw the production process from acquiring materials through to shipping the completed product. Throughout this process, maintaining open communication with the client to adjust deadlines and changes in product requirements. During my time in these industries, I found my love for creating solutions. I feel that nothing brings that knowledge of production and passion for problem solving together quite like software development.
